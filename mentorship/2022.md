The 2022 CompChemURG mentorship scheme aimed to match underrepresented members of the computational community with a mentor as part of a 3 month scheme, which included a mentorship workshop. 

The presentation for the mentorship scheme can be downloaded \extlink{here}{https://drive.google.com/file/d/1WHz9hOp3eFsdywDZM0Et0DZWNqrQDumo/view?usp=sharing}.

**Please note:** Applications for the 2022 mentorship scheme are now closed.
