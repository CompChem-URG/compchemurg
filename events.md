# Events & Meetings
CompChemURG hosts a number of events dedicated to supporting underrepresented groups and allies in Computational Chemistry. Our meetings aim to provide a safe, inclusive space for networking, mentorship, and sharing experiences across academia and industry. We believe that diversity enriches our scientific community, and our events feature speakers from a wide variety of backgrounds sharing their unique career pathways, research, and perspectives on navigating the field.
